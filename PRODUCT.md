# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential employers, collaborators, and clients evaluating the person behind the work. They come to understand not just what Fandi has built, but who he is — his thinking, his craft, and whether he'd be someone they'd genuinely enjoy working with.

## Product Purpose

A digital home, not a gallery. The site presents a frontend engineer's work and character through narrative, editorial craft, and quiet confidence. Success is visitors thinking "this feels thoughtful" and leaving with a sense of trust in the person, not just admiration for the interface.

## Brand Personality

Editorial-tech with warmth. Thoughtful, capable, genuine, calm, focused, intentional, timeless, approachable. The voice is measured and reflective — never hype-driven or self-promotional.

## Anti-references

- Cyberpunk aesthetics
- Startup dashboard visuals
- Overly futuristic interfaces
- High-energy creative agency styles
- AI-generated avatars
- Corporate headshots
- Cartoon illustrations
- Dramatic photo edits
- Lifestyle influencer aesthetics

## Design Principles

1. **Show the person, not the portfolio** — visitors should feel they're meeting someone, not evaluating a brand. If they remember the interface more than the person behind it, the design has failed.
2. **Editorial precision with warmth** — intellectual rigor of good publications balanced with genuine human connection. Not cold, not corporate.
3. **Quiet confidence** — no spectacle, no novelty for its own sake. Trust is earned through restraint and craft, not flashy effects.
4. **Content as narrative** — every project is a story about decisions, trade-offs, and outcomes, not just a screenshot with links. The telling matters as much as the doing.
5. **Authenticity above all** — the design serves the person, never the other way around.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Semantic HTML, full keyboard navigation with visible focus indicators, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for all animations, sufficient color contrast throughout, and meaningful alt text on all images.
